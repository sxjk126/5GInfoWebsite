What is 5G NR?

5G NR stands for 5G New Radio. It is claimed to be the universal standard for a unified more capable 5G wireless air interface. It will deliver significantly faster and more responsive mobile broadband experiences, and extend mobile technology to connect and redefine a multitude of new industries.
